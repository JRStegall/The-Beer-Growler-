HOW IT WORKS

We sell 32oz and 64 oz growlers, the bottle itself is a one time purchase of $4.99


we have 48 taps of craft beer, ciders and craft soda for you to choose from.


once you have finished your tasty BevErage simply rinse out the growler and bring it back into our store to fill again or trade for a growler token until you are ready for another fill.


the bottle sizes are interchangeable at no extra charge.


we clean and sanitize all returned bottles and give you a clean sanitized growler in exchange for yours.


we are happy to fill other retailers growlers as well. simply bring it in and we will clean and sanitize it on the spot, then fill it with a beverage of your choice.


when returning your growler to the store please rinse it thoroughly or leave about an inch of water in the bottom to avoid residue build up.
